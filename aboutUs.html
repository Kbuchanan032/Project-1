Our Mission

Have you ever found yourself in a new place unsure about what to do or where to have a drink? We have, which is why we set out to create an app that would take the hastle out of googling and searching for a bar or event, and we did just that. With the single click of a button we made it easy to to find the nearest restaurants, bars, or nature hikes so that you can stop worrying about what to do and get out and actually do.
